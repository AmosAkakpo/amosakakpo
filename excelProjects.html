2.Project2: Bike buying patterns/Dashboard

Welcome to my Excel project on bike buying patterns! This project aims to explore the relationship between people's demographic characteristics, such as gender, income, and number of children, and how often they buy bikes. By analyzing data from a survey of bike buyers, I have created an interactive tool that allows you to see how different factors affect bike purchasing behavior. Whether you're a bike enthusiast or just curious about consumer behavior, this project offers valuable insights into what motivates people to buy bikes. So take a look around, play with the interactive features, and discover something new about bike buying patterns!
